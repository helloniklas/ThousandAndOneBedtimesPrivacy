Privacy Policy for Thousand & One Bedtimes

Thousand & One Bedtimes (“the App”) is designed to provide personalised bedtime stories using on-device machine learning. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.
Specifically, the App does not collect:
	•	Personal information
	•	Contact information
	•	Identifiers
	•	Location data
	•	Usage data
	•	Diagnostics
	•	Photos, audio, or any other media
	•	Any information generated inside the App

All story generation and processing happen entirely on your device. No data leaves your device at any time.

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, or external APIs that collect data.

3. Children’s Privacy

Thousand & One Bedtimes is safe for use by children. Because we do not collect any personal information, we comply with all applicable children’s privacy laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

4. Data Retention

We retain no data.
Any stories, inputs, or preferences remain stored locally on the device and are deleted when the user deletes the App.

5. Security

Because we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no personal information for us to secure on remote servers.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we change this Privacy Policy in the future, we will update the date at the top of this page. Any changes will remain consistent with our commitment to not collect user data unless clearly communicated.
