Privacy Policy

Thousand & One Bedtimes — Last updated: November 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Thousand & One Bedtimes mobile application (the “App”) handles information. The App is designed to generate bedtime stories on-device and is intended to collect no personal data.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or diagnostics
  • Photos, audio, video, or other media
  • Any input or output data from the App is processed and retained only on the user’s device

2. On‑Device Processing

All story generation and machine learning processing occur entirely on the user's device. No inputs, outputs, or usage information are sent to our servers or to third parties.

3. Third‑Party Services

The App does not use third‑party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external APIs that collect user data.

4. Children’s Privacy

Because the App collects no personal information, it is safe for use by children and complies with applicable children’s privacy laws (for example, COPPA in the United States) as implemented by not collecting personal data.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain no user data on our servers. Any content generated or saved in the App remains on the device and will be removed if the user uninstalls the App or deletes the content locally.

6. Security

Because we do not collect or transmit personal information, there is no personal data stored on our servers to secure. Users are responsible for the security of the device and any local backups they use.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we decide to collect any personal data in the future, we will update this policy, clearly describe what data is collected and why, and provide users with the ability to opt in or opt out as required by law.
